The Most Powerful Marketing Tool You Are Not Using Enough

Client testimonial videos are consistently among the highest-performing content in any marketing mix. Potential customers trust the words of existing customers far more than any brand-produced messaging — and a well-produced testimonial video communicates that trust powerfully and authentically.

FilmGeneva specialises in producing testimonial videos that feel genuine, not scripted. We use a structured but conversational interview approach that draws out natural, credible responses — letting your clients tell their own story in their own words, without reading from a teleprompter or reciting pre-approved lines.

We have produced testimonial videos for clients across a wide range of industries — technology, professional services, NGOs, luxury brands, hospitality, and more. We know how to make interview subjects feel comfortable on camera and how to edit their responses into a compelling, concise narrative.

  • Professional lighting and audio at your client's location
  • Structured interview guide to draw out authentic responses
  • B-roll filming of your client in their environment
  • Editing with branded lower thirds, logos, and colour grading
  • Multiple cut lengths — 90-second full plus 30-second social cuts
  • Subtitles for silent autoplay on LinkedIn and Instagram
  • All formats for website, social media, and presentations

Why Professional Production Matters for Testimonials

A poorly lit, badly recorded testimonial actually damages trust — it signals a lack of investment and professionalism. A beautifully produced testimonial, on the other hand, elevates both the speaker and your brand.

FilmGeneva delivers testimonial videos that are polished enough to build credibility, but natural enough to feel authentic. That balance is harder to achieve than it looks — and it is where our experience makes the difference.

Whether you're an international broadcaster filming in Geneva for the first time, a brand running a multi-location campaign across Switzerland, or an organisation managing a complex event, we coordinate everything around your production so you can focus on the creative result.

  • Location scouting — finding and securing the right settings across Geneva and Switzerland
  • Obtaining permits — filming permits for public spaces, roads, and restricted locations
  • Hiring crew — assembling camera operators, sound recordists, lighting technicians, and production assistants
  • Renting equipment — access to specialty cameras, lenses, rigs, and broadcast gear beyond our own kit
  • Set coordination — scheduling, call sheets, and on-the-day logistics across every department

Location Scouting

Hiring Crew
