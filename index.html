Common questions

FAQ

How much does video production cost in Geneva? +

Rates vary depending on scope, duration, and complexity. We offer competitive pricing tailored to your needs — contact us for a free custom quote and we'll come back to you quickly.

What types of video do you produce? +

A full range: corporate video, event coverage, documentaries, commercials, podcast recording, livestreaming, drone footage, photography, CEO interviews, music videos, real estate films, science communication, NGO content, social media videos, and more.

Do you work outside Geneva? +

Yes. While based in Geneva, we regularly work across Switzerland — Zurich, Lausanne, Basel, Fribourg — and internationally for NGOs, corporations, and media organisations.

How quickly can you deliver? +

For simple shoots, edited content can be delivered within a few days. For larger productions, we agree on a clear timeline upfront. We're known for fast, reliable delivery.

Do you work with NGOs and international organisations? +

Absolutely. We have extensive experience with NGOs, UN agencies, and international organisations in Geneva — events, testimonials, fundraising films, field documentation, and annual reports.

What camera and equipment do you use? +

Professional cinema cameras up to 6K (Sony FX6/FX9), licensed drone, lavalier and condenser microphones, LED lighting kits, gimbals, and broadcast-grade livestreaming setups.

Can you handle photography and video in the same shoot? +

Yes — we regularly combine video and photography in a single efficient shoot, saving you time and budget while ensuring visual consistency across all deliverables.

Do you offer social media video content? +

Yes. We produce Reels, Shorts, and branded social content optimised for Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, and YouTube — in all required formats and aspect ratios.

Who we are

OUR STORY

FilmGeneva is a professional video production company based in Geneva, Switzerland — and a trusted production partner for international broadcasters, agencies, and organisations filming in the region.

We offer a full range of services: corporate video, event filming, livestreaming, photography, podcast recording, and drone footage. But we also function as a full-service production company — handling everything beyond just the camera.

Geneva is one of the world's most important cities for international organisations, diplomacy, and innovation. We have built deep experience working in this environment — from high-profile UN events and NGO campaigns to Swiss corporate productions, luxury brands, and independent creative projects.

We work with clients of all sizes: multinational corporations, UN agencies, NGOs, universities, startups, artists, and individuals. Previous clients include the BBC, ITV, WHO, WTO, CERN, ICRC, AI for Good, TikTok, Campus Biotech, the Swiss Economic Forum, the University of Geneva, and many more.

We are known for competitive pricing, fast turnaround, and the ability to adapt professionally to any environment — whether that's a critical international conference, a luxury brand shoot, or an intimate personal project.
